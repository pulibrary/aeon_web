Contacting the Library

Special Collections Research Accounts are used in the Department of Special Collections in the Firestone Library, the Seeley G. Mudd Library, the Marquand Library of Art and Archaeology, and the East Asian Library. Questions should be directed to the library that you plan to visit:

  • Special Collections, Firestone Library
    • Ask Us! or (609) 258-3184
    • https://library.princeton.edu/special-collections/
  • Seeley G. Mudd Manuscript Library
    • mudd@princeton.edu or (609) 258-6345
    • https://library.princeton.edu/special-collections/mudd
  • Marquand Library of Art and Archaeology
    • marquand@princeton.edu or (609) 258-3783
    • https://library.princeton.edu/marquand/services/rare-book-use
  • East Asian Library and the Gest Collection
    • mheijdra@princeton.edu or gest@princeton.edu or (609) 258-5336
    • https://library.princeton.edu/eastasian/rare-books
